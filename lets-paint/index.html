Instructions

No paint brushes? No paint? Thousand of miles away from your friends? No worries because "Let's Paint a Flower" fixes all of that!

You and all your friends each get a paint brush and can choose it's size and color to paint a flower together. If you want to save your masterpiece, click on the "save image" button. You can paint as many flowers as you like with the use of the "clear" button.

You are all set now to go on paint!
